Publication Design, 2016

A catalogue showcasing ten of our favourite books. The publication was exhibited at Offprint Paris, Autumn 2016. Collaboration with graphic design students Zuzanna Zgierska, Rhodé Tavenier, Jungyeon Sung and Beatrice Borgström at the Royal Academy of Art, The Hague.

Foreplay

Concept & Poster Design, 2016

Poster series for a performative club night at the Contemporary Art Museum of Estonia (EKKM), Tallinn. The night combined deejay sets, live visuals & video footage filmed by Helsinki based artist Man Yau. Concept in collaboration with a team.
Dialogue

Animation, 2017

In the age of mass surveillance we’ve given away the authorship of our digital selves. Are we still able to share the most intimate matters with our friends, or have we started to filter ourselves without us even noticing it? Sound by Vesa-Matti Kivioja.
